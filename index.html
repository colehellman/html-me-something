My Coding Journey
How I Got Here

A friend's suggestion to get involved with Launchcode helped me discover I have a passion for programming. Before I joined Launchcode I had no idea what I wanted to do with my professional career. I knew I loved technology, & I wanted it to be a big part of my job. Launchcode showed me how to accomplish this goal.

Launchcode provides many learning opportunities. I have taken multiple classes from Launchcode. There also groups to participate in once you class is over with. My ultimate goal with Launchcode is to get a web developer job through their mentorship program. Here are the programs I have done through Launchcode:

  • Hello, World
  • Summer of Code
  • LC101
  • Mentorship Tuesdays
  • Launchcode candidate

I am very grateful for Launchcode, & the opportunities it has afforded me. Without Launchcode, I would most likely still have no idea what I wanted to do for a career. Launchcode has helped give me direction & a tangible goal. I will continue my learning journey with Launchcode. I recommend anyone with an interest in coding to see what they have to offer.
